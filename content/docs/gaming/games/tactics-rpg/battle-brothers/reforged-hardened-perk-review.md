# Battle Brothers Reforged, Hardened Perk Review

## General

### Bags and Belts

**Power Level**: D

I almost never take this perk.
But I could see it being useful if you want to have a bro with many high-weight
weapons (perhaps a weapon master?).
Even then, there are probably better fatigue-saving perks.

## Axe

**Power Level**: A

Axes seem really good in hardened.
They are really versatile and seem to work well against all enemy types.

### Dismemberment

**Power Level**: B

I think injuries can be really powerful.
Often at the highest tier if you injure an enemy you can basically treat them as
dead and ignore them for the rest of the fight.
This perk makes that happen more often.

Of course there is rng associated with what injury you get.

**Use Cases:**

 - Build 1: <description>

**Synergies:**

 - Crippling Strikes

### Axe Mastery

**Power Level**: A

Fatigue reduction is really important.

Disarm is also really good.

For low matk bros, getting guaranteed dazes on shield strikes is really useful
as well. This can unlock a qatal duelist or just help tanks tank longer.

### Dismantle

**Power Level**: B

Lots of late game enemies have a lot of armor.

Shield damage is less useful.

### Cleaver Mastery

**Power Level**: A

Fatigue reduction is really important.

### Sanguinary

**Power Level**: D

Bleeding as an effect is kinda meh.
It would be different if the bleeding happened at the start of a turn instead of
at the end, or if it did more damage per stack.

## Flail

**Power Level**: A

### Whirling Death

**Power Level**: A

Good for tanks to spend AP.
Useful in general, pretty easy to start spinning right before making contact
with enemies.
Better on less mobile bros to make sure you have AP to use this instead of
walking.

### Flail Mastery

**Power Level**: A

Fatigue reduction is really important.

### Flail Spinner

**Power Level**: A

More damage!
Especially good at proccing additional effects.
Perhaps particularly good with poisons?

## Knave

### Cheap Trick

**Power Level**: D

I have never taken this perk.
It's probably good with whips to guarantee a disarm.
But I think in general there are much more efficient ways to get better hit
chance via reach, better bros, etc.

I could see this being useful on a low mATK bro with a lot of fatigue cost
reduction perks, plus double grip.  But I haven't tried this.
