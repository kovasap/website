# Battle Brothers Reforged, Hardened Build Archetypes

## Template

Overview.

### Stats

Stats required for the build.  Legend:

 - ++: As high as possible, critical for the build
 - +: Nice to prioritize, but don't need crazy high values
 - -: Not really necessary for the build to work
 - --: We actively want this to be low, or really don't care about it

If stats are not listed, they can be ignored.

## Ghostlike Executioner

Basically, run up to an enemy and kill them.  Then back off and recover.
This brother should really not be sitting in melee for an extended time.
In some fights, they might do well with a 2h reach weapon.

Can build as a flaker with vigorous assault and dodge.
But this is riskier than using rotation and putting them in the backline.

### Stats

Really forgiving build stat wise.

 - mAtk+
 - mDef-
 - Fat-
 - Res-
 - Ini+

### Perks

 - Core:
   - Ghostlike
   - Fresh and Furious
   - Formidable Approach
   - Vigorous Assault
   - Rotation
 - Nice to have:
   - Double Strike
   - Flexible
   - Axe Perks

## Headhunter

Frontline build, but could also be used as a more fragile double grip damage
dealer.

Use a 1h flail to get a headshot, then switch to a 1h axe to finish them off.

Important to have a way to manage fatigue costs.

### Stats

 - mAtk+
 - mDef+
 - Fat+
 - Res+

### Perks

 - Core:
   - Flail Perks
   - Axe Perks
   - Weapon Master
   - Quick Hands
   - Headhunter
   - Battle Flow
   - Double Strike
 - Nice to have:
   - Duelist
   - Finesse

## Lone Wolf Flanker

Separate from the rest of the squad to hunt down backline units like
necromancers, priests, witches, bosses, etc.

Can also kite away some enemies to get them out of the fight.

Don't draw too many enemies!

### Stats

 - rAtk++
 - mAtk+
 - Fat+
 - Ini+

### Perks

 - Core:
   - Lone Wolf
   - Pathfinder
   - Dodge (especially useful for the rDef)
   - Flexible / Nimble
   - Hybridization
   - Bow Perks
 - Nice to have:
   - Weapon Master
   - AoE 2h weapon perks.
