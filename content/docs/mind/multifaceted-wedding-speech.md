# Multifaceted Wedding Speech

*A speech I gave at my sister's wedding:*

As we all know, People are multifaceted creatures.
We are the sum of decades of lived experience, compounded upon the experience of
people we share the world with.
And within our minds these experiences have shaped not a single entity, but a
network of different desires, habits, and abilities.

It’s little wonder then that the ways we interact can be so complex, as these
different aspects of ourselves play off each other.
Sometimes harmonizing, sometimes clashing.

Marriage provides an excellent opportunity to focus and manage all these aspects
of ourselves.
Your spouse is uniquely positioned to see your own facets from a new vantage, as
you see theirs.
If they notice something new in you, listen to them.
Don’t be afraid to introspect together to find the source of their observation.
Their view of you is truly one of a kind.

My hope is that you will continue to learn and grow together, and be delighted
by each new version of yourselves, as you navigate the next chapters of your
lives together.
Congratulations.
